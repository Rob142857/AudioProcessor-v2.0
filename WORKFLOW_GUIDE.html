🎙️ Audio Transcription Workflow

From Raw Audio to Archive-Ready Document

1

🎵 Automated Transcription

Run your audio or video file through the Speech-to-Text Transcription Tool. The AI-powered Whisper model processes the audio and generates a word-for-word verbatim transcription with intelligent punctuation.

📁
Input
Audio/Video File (.mp3, .wav, .mp4, .m4a, etc.)
🎯 Quality Mode 🧠 Whisper AI 📝 Auto-Punctuation 🔤 Special Words Recognition ⚡ GPU Accelerated
2

👁️ Human Review & Enhancement

The human editor listens to the original audio while reviewing the DOCX output. This critical step ensures accuracy and transforms the raw transcription into a polished, publication-ready document.

📄
Working With
Generated DOCX Transcript + Original Audio
  • Listen to audio and verify word-for-word accuracy
  • Correct any AI transcription errors or mishearings
  • Refine punctuation for natural reading flow
  • Add question marks (?) to identify when questions are asked
  • Insert images, pictures, and diagrams where appropriate
  • Format paragraphs and add section headings
  • Verify proper nouns and technical terminology
  • Add speaker identification if multiple speakers
🎧 Audio Comparison ✏️ Error Correction ❓ Question Marking 🖼️ Image Insertion 📐 Formatting
3

📚 Archive-Ready Document

The final polished document is now publication-ready and suitable for archiving. It preserves the exact spoken words while presenting them in a professional, readable format that can be easily searched, referenced, and distributed.

✨
Final Output
Polished DOCX / PDF / eBook (Calibre-compatible)
  • Verbatim word-for-word accuracy verified by human
  • Professional punctuation and formatting
  • Questions clearly marked for easy identification
  • Images and visual aids integrated
  • Ready for Calibre eBook conversion
  • Suitable for long-term archival storage
  • Searchable and indexable text
✓ Publication Ready

🎯 Quality Assurance Philosophy

This workflow combines the speed and consistency of AI transcription with the irreplaceable accuracy of human verification. The AI handles the heavy lifting of initial transcription, while human expertise ensures every word is correct, every question is marked, and the final document meets archival standards.
